Movie Webpage Project

Context

With a partner, we were required to make a movie web project about a fictional movie that would allow us to practice our HTML and CSS concepts. Me and my partner decided to do comedy with a meaningful theme behind it, and the title for our movie was "Bear's eating contest.

Content

For our project, we first needed to make a plan. We divided up the work between us and decided to write about a bears eating contest. To sum up our story, it's basically about a big and small bear that participate in an eating contest and the big bear wins but then he realizes that winning is not always the most imporant thing. Our story was meant to be fun and thoughtful, sharing a nice message behind it.

Reflection

Some challenges me and my partner faced while making this project was trying to center images since if we had other padding or margin in the same selector, it would interfere and our images wouldn't be centered. From this challenge, I was able to learn how to overcome it again and take away the idea that if I ever experience my image not centering again.

For my next steps, I would've probably added onto the story more if I had the time, but the priority was the code.
